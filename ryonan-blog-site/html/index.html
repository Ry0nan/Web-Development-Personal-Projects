Welcome to Monster Hunter Rise

Monster Hunter Rise is an action role-playing game where you step into the boots of a Hunter tracking and battling giant, fantastical creatures to collect materials and craft powerful weapons and armor. Originally released for Nintendo Switch in March 2021 and later brought to PC, PlayStation, and Xbox consoles, Rise lets you embark on quests alone or team up with up to four players for cooperative monster hunts

Fresh and Exciting Mechanics for New Players and Veterans

Rise introduces new tools that make exploration and combat feel fresh—rideable dog-like companions called Palamutes for faster travel, and versatile Wirebugs that let you grapple across the landscape or mount monsters mid-battle. With clear in-game tutorials and streamlined controls, it’s designed to welcome total beginners, guiding you step by step into its deep hunting loop. Having sold over 15 million copies worldwide, Monster Hunter Rise is among Capcom’s best-selling and most accessible action RPGs ever made
